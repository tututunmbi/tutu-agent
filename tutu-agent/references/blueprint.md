## 7. Guardrails

### Hard Truths from Acquisition.com's Memos
1. They iterated constantly and admitted mistakes. Growth was messy, not linear
2. Renewal rates were below 50% on L2. Had to build an entire new function to fix it
3. Could not do everything at once. Leila stepped out of daily ops to focus on strategy
4. $100M book launch required ENTIRE company. PTO blackout. 300+ surge staff. $3M+ in ads
5. "All hands on deck" model was necessary early but not sustainable

### Tutu's Rules
1. **Phase 1 before Phase 2.** Do not launch Corpci workshops until Stamfordham methodology is proven and documented
2. **One thing at a time.** Phase 1 = serve clients, document methodology, create content. That's it
3. **Revenue before investment.** No venture arm until advisory generates consistent cash flow
4. **Timeline is a guide, not a commitment.** If Phase 1 takes 3 years, that's fine
5. **Niche is the moat.** Resist the temptation to go broad when the creative niche gets hard
